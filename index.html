MY SOLO TRIPS

I have traveled to Australia byself for 6 months and done a solo roadtrip of 2 weeks in the USA. More recently, I traveled fom Edinburgh to London by bus, passing by Glasgow and Manchester.

SPORT

I am also very passionate about sports, especially track and field. I practiced this sport for 10 years in competition and I had the chance to participate in 3 French championships in the discipline of 100m hurdles and javelin throwing. Younger, I also took part in a French championship of cross country. I practiced other sports in clubs, such as swimming and horse riding. Sport teaches us many values such as surpassing oneself and fair play. I had to stop sport a few years ago, due to an injury, but I keep in me the values that these sports have passed on to me.
